Bicycle Service Centre

We service and repair all types, makes and models of bicycle in the Cardiff, Caerphilly and wider South Wales area.

We have a wide range of spare parts in stock from Shimano to Campagnolo. Whether it is a puncture, full bike build or a chat about your next upgrade/purchase we are here to help.

We can collect your bike or you can deliver it to us, we can turn around jobs while you wait or you can leave it with us.
